Explore.AT!

Available Visualizations

The following examples have been developed and tested in Google Chrome, and therefore this is the recommended browser to explore them.

Geographical Lemmas
Geographical exploration of Lemmas, with filtering options (text, part of speech and year) enabled to highlight only certain words in the map.
Word-Source Relations
Geographical exploration of Lemmas, including filtering and relations between words based on the source they appear in when clicking on any of them.
Lemma Context Navigation
Generate an image-aided treemap containing the most meaningful contexts where the searched lemma appears, and explore the occurences.
Raw Questionnaire Exploration
Generate dynamic HTML based hierarchies with Questionnaires that hold Questions that reference Lemmas.
Hierarchical Questionnaire Tree
Dynamic tree generation of Questionnaire hierarchies based on the content matching searches.
Circle Packed Questionnaires
Questionnaire exploration through a hierarchy of packed circles which highlight relations between questions and lemmas.
Bedeutung Prototype
This is a prototype to explore the BD field
TUSTEP Map
TUSTEP Spatio-Temporal Analysis Tool
Questionnaire Explorer
Multi-view Questioner Explorer
RDF Plants Explorer
Scientific and Common Plant name explorer based on RDF data and connected to Europeana
